LIS 598 - Data Curation II

Chapter 6 Data Packaging

Overview: As data grow in size, and the documentation, software, and dependencies of an operating system grow in complexity, it is becoming increasingly hard to access data without a conceptual “package” to do so. In this week we’re going to think about how both the metaphor and the practical application of “packaging” impacts data curation work. This is very much an emerging topic so if it seems like a somewhat fuzzy idea - that is because it absolutely is… But, my hope is that thinking through data packaging will also help us begin to see how and why our work in developing metadata for data curation is paramount to sustainable access. The readings this week reflect the emergence of this topic. I would like you to choose one of the standards I’ve listed to do some reading of technical documentation.
